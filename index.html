Research Interests

Stellar Activity

Investigating the dynamic processes that shape stellar surfaces and their impact on exoplanet detection.

Exoplanets

Developing innovative techniques for exoplanet detection and characterization, pushing the boundaries of our understanding of alien worlds.

Optical Physics

Designing and optimizing astrophotonic systems to enhance our observational capabilities and unlock new cosmic discoveries.
